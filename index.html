Video Game Music Unit


In the Video Game Music Unit, we will be exploring computer-generated sound, chiptunes, sound waves, as well as the relation between video game music and movie scores, sound-design, and theme and variation. In this unit, students will have the opportunity to create their own idea for a video game, including a plot/ story, visual representation, and, of course, music! New this year, students will have the opportunity to complete this project on their own, with one other person, or with two other people.
