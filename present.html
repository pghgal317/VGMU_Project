Presentation


You have been hired by a video game company to create a new, original video game concept for 4th & 5th graders!
Your idea should be:

  • School and age appropriate
  • Interesting
  • Creative
  • and a YOU / YOUR TEAM original


In your presentation, incorporate your music, artwork, and tell us the story behind your game.
Imagine that you are presenting to potential investors or employers (Shark Tank, Amazon, etc.)

Your presentation should include:

  • Story behind your game/ characters
  • Music demonstration
  • Artwork presentation/ demonstration
  • Any extra-challenge options

Feel free to create a slide-deck using Google Slides for your presentation. By doing so, you can incorporate all of your components (music, digital art, story, etc) into one place. You can create slides on your own or click here for slide templates that Ms. C. created. Feel free to add, remove, or edit these slides as needed! Click "Make a Copy" when prompted to copy this template into your own Google Drive.


Follow the directions here to see how to share and submit your Google Slide Deck (with a couple of reminders on how to embed your music as well!). Let Ms. C. know if you have questions or need any help!
